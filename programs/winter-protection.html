Why Winter Protection is Critical

Winter dormancy doesn't mean your lawn stops needing care. In fact, fall treatments are most important for ensuring your lawn survives harsh Massachusetts winters and emerges strong in spring. Without proper protection, your lawn may winterkill or spring slowly with weak, thin turf.
