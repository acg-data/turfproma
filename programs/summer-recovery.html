Why Summer Recovery is Essential

Summer takes a toll on Massachusetts lawns. Heat stress, drought conditions, foot traffic, and insect activity can leave turf weak, thin, and vulnerable to fall disease. Summer Recovery treatment reverses summer damage and primes lawn for optimal fall growth.

  • Repairs heat and drought damage
  • Prepares roots for fall growth
  • Eliminates late-season pests
  • Restores overall lawn health
  • Reduces fall disease risk
