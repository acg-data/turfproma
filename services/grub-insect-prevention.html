Common Lawn Pests in Massachusetts

Understanding which pests threaten your lawn is the first step in protection. Our local expertise means we know exactly what to look for and how to treat it effectively.

Grubs

Larvae of Japanese beetles and other insects that eat grass roots, causing brown, dying patches that can be peeled up like carpet.

Chinch Bugs

Tiny insects that suck sap from grass blades, causing yellowing, browning, and dead patches in sunny areas.

Sod Webworms

Moth larvae that create webbed tunnels in grass, feeding on blades and causing irregular brown patches.
